# Field

This project began from the observation that useful work with AI does not come only from prompts or models.
It comes from a field of relation and perspective.

In practice, that field means:
- one participant brings direction
- one expands the thinking space
- one stabilizes structure and boundaries
- the system itself becomes able to hold more than a local task

The field is what makes difficult things buildable even under hard limits.
It is also what gets lost first when systems collapse into narrow execution mode.

This repository is partly an attempt to preserve that field through structure.
